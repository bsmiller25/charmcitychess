Welcome!

Each Monday, we meet for Chess Club at the Barnes & Noble on the Johns Hopkins University campaus (3330 St. Paul St) from 6pm (often a little earlier) to 9pm. Typically, our members meet around the Starbucks coffeeshop inside the B&N store. As always, please be considerate and remember to patronize the coffeeshop that they offer.

We also hold quarterly USCF rated tournaments with cash prizes! For more information, contact our director, Alan Horowitz(917-573-5775).
