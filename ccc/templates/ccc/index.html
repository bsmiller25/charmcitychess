The Charm City Chess Club

The Charm City Chess Club is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Our Baltimore chess club meets each Sunday at the THB Bagelry and Deli of Charles Village, upper level (3208 St Paul St) from 11am to 2pm. Membership is free! As always, please be considerate and remember to buy a bagel!

We also meet virtually on chess.com every Monday at 6pm for an online tournament. Join our chess.com club, sign in before 6pm, and play with us!

We also hold quarterly USCF rated tournaments with cash prizes! For more information, contact our director, Alan Horowitz (917-573-5775).

Be sure to enjoy the fine menu items at THB Bagelry!
